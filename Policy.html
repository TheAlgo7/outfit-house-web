Exchange & Shipping

The Rules.
Plain & Simple.

No hidden conditions. No fine print designed to trip you up. Here's exactly how exchanges, shipping, and payments work at The Outfit House.

Exchange Policy

We offer a free size exchange on all unworn, undamaged pairs within 7 days of delivery. If the size isn't right, message us on WhatsApp with a few photos and we'll sort it.

  • Exchange window: 7 days from date of delivery
  • Condition: unworn and undamaged, in original packaging
  • Process: WhatsApp photos of the pair → we confirm availability → swap dispatched
  • Subject to stock availability at the time of exchange

We do not offer full monetary returns. Because we photograph and confirm every pair on WhatsApp before dispatch, buyers have the opportunity to verify the product before payment is finalised. This is why we require photo confirmation before we ship — it protects both sides.

Shipping

We ship PAN-India via trusted courier partners. Every order is dispatched in plain, unmarked outer packaging — no branding, no visible indication of contents.

  • Delivery time: 2–5 business days nationwide
  • Delhi NCR: same-day dispatch possible for orders confirmed before 1pm
  • Real tracking number shared on WhatsApp once dispatched
  • Discreet outer packaging — no logos or product names on box
  • Shipping is free on all orders

Payment

We accept the following payment methods. Payment details are shared after order confirmation on WhatsApp.

  • UPI — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, any UPI app
  • Bank Transfer — IMPS / NEFT to our account
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) — available in most metro cities; some pin codes require prepaid due to high RTO rates

QC Photos Before Dispatch

Every pair is opened and photographed in our New Delhi studio before it ships. You receive clear photos — box open, stitching close-up, sole, and any detail you ask for. You confirm before we dispatch. This is non-negotiable and protects you from receiving something you didn't approve.

Questions?

If anything here is unclear or your situation isn't covered, message us directly. We reply in under 10 minutes most of the day.
