Quick answers.

How do I order?
Send a WhatsApp message with the pair and size. We'll confirm stock, send live photos, and share a UPI/COD/bank transfer option.
How long is delivery?
2–5 business days PAN-India. Delhi NCR same-day in most cases. Discreet packaging, real tracking.
What if the size is wrong?
Free size exchange within 7 days on unworn pairs. Just message us with photos.
Do you accept returns?
Sizing exchange yes; full returns no — these are not stocked at retail volume. We send detailed photos before dispatch precisely so you can decide upfront.
Is COD available?
Yes, in most metros. Some pin codes need prepaid via UPI or bank transfer due to RTO rates.
