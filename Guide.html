Entry, Standard
or Vault?

Every piece we carry is hand-checked and graded into one of three tiers. The grade is the single most useful thing to understand before you message us, because it sets the build, the finish and the price. Here is exactly what changes between them, how to pick, and how ordering works.

What the tiers mean

We describe products by silhouette, material and colour, never by brand. The tier tells you how the piece is built and finished. There are no fixed prices online: you message us on WhatsApp, we send real photos of the exact piece, and we share the price then. We bargain, and we run launch offers often.

How to pick

Going by budget

If you want the lowest entry point for everyday rotation, Entry does the job and still passes a hand-check. If you want the piece most people will read as the real thing, Standard is where the value lives. If the detail has to be exact, go Vault.

Going by use

Daily beaters and gym-to-street wear suit Entry and Standard. Pieces you want to keep clean, photograph, or hold onto suit Vault. For accessories like wallets, belts and watches, the jump to Vault buys you materials and finish that hold up over years, not months.

Going by silhouette

Chunky runners, retro runners and court sneakers look excellent across all three tiers. High-tops, full-grain leather pieces and luxury accessories show the biggest difference at Vault, because that is where stitching and leather quality are most visible.

Sizing

Sneakers are listed in UK sizing and run true to the original silhouette. Apparel and bottomwear are unisex and cut from the original fit, so order your usual size. If you are between sizes or unsure, message us before you order and we will tell you straight whether to size up or down for that specific piece.

How ordering works

There is no checkout. You browse the catalog, message us on WhatsApp with the piece and tier, and we confirm stock. For anything not physically on our shelf, we arrange it on request, it usually reaches the store within the hour. We send real QC photos of your exact piece before you pay. Pay by UPI, bank transfer, or COD in most metros. We ship PAN-India, with the shipping cost shared on WhatsApp before you pay, packed discreet with zero branding on the outer box.

Common questions

Why are there no prices on the site?

Because we bargain and run frequent launch and seasonal offers, a fixed public number would only work against you. Message us and you will get the current best price along with real photos.

Is the Standard tier worth it over Entry?

For most buyers, yes. Standard moves you to a genuine leather upper and a stitched sole with original tooling, which is the jump most people actually notice. It is the tier we point first-time buyers to.

What do I get with Vault?

Premium full-grain leather, precision stitching, and full retail packaging. It is the closest match we offer, aimed at collectors and premium buyers.

Can I see the exact piece before paying?

Always. We photograph your specific piece and send it on WhatsApp. You confirm first, then we dispatch.
