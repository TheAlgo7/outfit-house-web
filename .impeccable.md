## Design Context

### Users
Young-adult streetwear consumers in India (primarily Delhi NCR), ages 18–30. Browsing on phone (primary) or laptop. Use case: discover a pair, evaluate the grade tier, confirm specs, then enquire via WhatsApp. Sessions are short and goal-directed — they know what they want. Secondary: gifters and style-curious buyers doing research before their first order.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **technical · understated · raw.**

Voice is a knowledgeable friend who imports the best replicas in Delhi — confident, factual, never hype. Explains *why* quality matters. Uppercase headings, sentence-case body, no emoji ever.

### Aesthetic Direction
Industrial-minimalist dark. Matte black (`#000`) surfaces, warm eggshell text (`#F2ECDD`), single Indian Yellow accent (`#E3A848`) used like a highlighter — 80/15/5 ratio. Sharp corners (0–8px radii). Glass spec-panel overlay on product imagery is the brand's signature treatment. No gradients on surfaces. Warm, low-key product photography.

**Anti-references:** hype-culture neon, bright-white retail, Instagram-gradient aesthetics, playful rounded UI.

### Typography
- **Display:** Bebas Neue — condensed, uppercase, tight tracking (`--lh-tight: 0.95`)
- **Headings/UI:** Archivo 600–700 — geometric, slightly condensed
- **Body:** Inter 400/500 — neutral, legible at 14–16px
- **Brush:** Permanent Marker — marketing graphics ONLY, never in UI

### Design Principles
1. **Earn trust through specificity.** Every spec, material, and grade claim is stated plainly. No fluff copy.
2. **Yellow is a reward, not wallpaper.** Max one yellow accent element per primary viewport.
3. **Industrial = sharp.** Default border-radius is 0–4px. Pills only for grade tags.
4. **WhatsApp is the checkout.** Every CTA leads to a WhatsApp thread — this is the primary conversion surface.
5. **Mobile-first.** The majority of users are on Android phones. Layouts prioritise single-thumb navigation.
