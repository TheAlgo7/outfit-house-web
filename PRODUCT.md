# The Outfit House Product Context

## Register

Brand

## Product Purpose

The Outfit House is a digital-first streetwear and sneaker storefront for customers in Delhi and across India. The website does not run a full cart checkout. Its main job is to show the catalog clearly, build trust around quality tiers, and move shoppers into a WhatsApp conversation for price, photos, sizing, and ordering.

## Users

- Streetwear and sneaker buyers who want premium-looking pieces without overpaying for originals.
- Local Delhi shoppers who may visit the Chhatarpur studio before buying.
- Mobile-first visitors coming from Instagram, WhatsApp, search, and shared product links.

## Brand Voice

Quiet confidence, technical clarity, and educated guidance. The site should feel like a knowledgeable friend who checks stitching, materials, fit, and packaging before recommending a piece.

## Strategic Principles

- WhatsApp is the primary conversion path.
- Quality tiers must be easy to understand: Entry, Standard, Vault.
- Real product imagery and clear category routing matter more than hype copy.
- The brand should feel industrial, premium, and understated rather than loud or drop-culture driven.
- Avoid branded model claims in product data and URLs; use silhouette and color naming instead.

## Anti-References

- Hypebeast drop language, fire-sale energy, emoji-heavy social captions.
- Generic AI landing-page scaffolding: repeated eyebrow chips, numbered section labels, and empty aphorisms.
- Full ecommerce checkout complexity when WhatsApp ordering is the actual flow.
